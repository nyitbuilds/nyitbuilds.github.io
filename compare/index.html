Student sites · a choice to make

The same site, two ways.

On the left, the site with the student’s own photo. On the right, the same site with a generic avatar, which is what every student site becomes if photos are not allowed. Scroll either one. The gap in warmth and credibility is the real cost of the safer-looking choice.

A way to keep both the appeal and the caution: students add their own photo to their own site after the event, with parental consent, and can remove it at any time. NYIT never hosts or controls a student’s image, so the institution carries no new liability while the student keeps the part that makes the page feel real.
